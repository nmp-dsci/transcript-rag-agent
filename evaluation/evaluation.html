Answer

The transcript states that the 50% capital gains tax discount has been abolished for new purchases, but existing properties are not fully grandfathered for CGT (unlike negative gearing). Instead, there is a transitional rule: you can obtain a valuation as at 30 June 2027 (or 1 July 2027) to lock in the 50% discount on gains up to that date; gains after that point move to an indexation model. Therefore, if you sell before 30 June 2027, you can still access the full 50% discount on the entire capital gain, because there is no post-valuation gain. If you sell after that date, only the pre-valuation portion gets the discount; the rest is indexed.
